About Raptors Club

The Raptors Club at MIT is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing aerospace innovation and fostering a passion for aviation and space exploration. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of aerospace engineers and enthusiasts.

We specialize in three core domains: Fixed Wing, Rotary Wing, and Rocketry. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions, we aim to push the boundaries of aerospace technology and solve real-world challenges.

Our Domains

Fixed Wing

Design and development of fixed-wing aircraft, focusing on aerodynamics, stability, and performance optimization.

Rotary Wing

Exploring the mechanics and applications of rotary-wing aircraft, including drones and helicopters.

Rocketry

Building and launching rockets, with a focus on propulsion systems, payload integration, and recovery mechanisms.
