About Us

The Raptors Club of MIT is a student organization dedicated to fostering innovation and knowledge in aerospace engineering. Our mission is to inspire and educate the next generation through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions.

We believe in collaboration and creativity to solve complex problems and push the boundaries of aerospace technology.
